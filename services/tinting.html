Services · Window Tinting

WINDOW TINTING

Premium carbon and ceramic film for heat rejection, UV protection, and a clean factory-style finish. Lifetime warranty on bubbling, peeling, and color fade.

Ceramic Tint

Our flagship film. Ceramic particles block up to 88% of infrared heat without going dark or interfering with electronics, GPS, or cell signal. Stays cool, stays crystal clear, and never fades to purple.

Carbon Tint

A great-looking, high-quality film at a more accessible price point. Solid heat rejection, deep matte finish, and complete UV blocking. Ideal for daily drivers.

Available Shades

From a subtle 50% factory-look up to a deep 5% limo tint. We'll walk you through California's legal limits and what's actually enforced — pick the shade that fits how you drive.
