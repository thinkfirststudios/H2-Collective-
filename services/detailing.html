Services · Detailing

DETAILING

A multi-stage process that goes deeper than a car wash — every surface decontaminated, polished, and protected. Walk away with paint that looks better than the day you drove it home.

The Process

We start with a controlled hand wash, decontamination with iron remover and clay, and a thorough inspection under high-output lighting. From there, the path branches depending on your goal — light enhancement, full paint correction, or a long-term protective coating.

Interiors get the same treatment: steam-extracted carpets, deep-cleaned leather, and trim brought back to factory finish. Every package finishes with a final inspection so nothing leaves until it's right.

What's Included

  • Two-bucket hand wash with pH-neutral shampoo
  • Iron decontamination and clay bar treatment
  • Single-stage paint enhancement (signature & premium packages)
  • Multi-stage paint correction (premium package)
  • Interior steam clean, vacuum, and leather conditioning
  • Wheel & wheel-well deep clean
  • Tire dressing, trim restoration, glass treatment
  • Optional ceramic coating add-on (1-yr, 3-yr, or 5-yr durability)

Ceramic Coatings

Our ceramic coatings cure to a hydrophobic, scratch-resistant layer that protects your paint for years. Choose between our 1-year sealant for daily drivers, the 3-year coating for everyday protection, or our flagship 5-year coating for show-car-level depth and gloss.
